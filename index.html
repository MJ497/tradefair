About The Trade-A-Thon

The Calabar Trade-A-Thon, the first of its kind in Calabar, is a groundbreaking trade fair focused on empowering MSMEs for a sustainable future. This dynamic event promotes innovation, collaboration, and growth, bringing together entrepreneurs, businesses, and stakeholders to foster opportunities and showcase the potential of South-South Nigeria's thriving MSME sector.
